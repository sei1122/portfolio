Loyalty Program

Modernized the user interface of the loyalty member platform,
including the dashboard, login page, and site, for a fresh look.

Overview

Following its acquisition by Tata Group, Air India required a design refresh for its loyalty program to reflect the airline's revitalized brand and future direction. Our team focused on redesigning key areas, including the member logged-in pages, the main loyalty consumer site, and loyalty partnership pages.

YEAR

2023-2024, 6 month


ROLE

UX/UI Design

TEAM

Seiko (self), Keri (Lead), Joanne (UX/UI), PMs, Project Managers, Developers, Loyalty team


TOOLS

Figma

Consumer site

Provides members with tools and information to manage their accounts, earn and redeem miles, understand program benefits, and access exclusive offers.

Member Login

Provide loyalty members with a centralized view of their points balance, recent account activity, and current membership tier status upon login.

Partnership Page

Showcase monthly partner deals that allow loyalty members to earn additional points.
