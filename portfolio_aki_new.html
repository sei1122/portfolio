Blue Palette

This is an award-winning latte artist, Aki Tokuhara’s website (Blue Palette is her company). Aki makes latte art for a commercial poster, TV and company event. She also holds a latte art workshop for adults and kids. My work was to create a logo, design and develop a website.

LOGO PROCESS

Aki has an idea for Blue Pallett’s logo and she made a logo draft. My part was to bring the draft to the Illustrator and refining the logo; selecting colors, changing the typography and adding the line texture. The logo creation became the starting point of the brand and this gave the identity for the brand.

INITIAL IDEA

This image is the initial draft from Aki. Her idea of the logo is a blue palette with the company’s initial “BP” and the hole for the holing palette part which is also implying the shape of the cup.

REFINE THE LOGO

First I drew the logo based on Aki’s draft, and I changed the color for each wood grid like blue gradation. Then I change the logo font and the color on the front to more unified. At last, we changed the line’s texture to pencil texture to add a more crafty impression.

WEBSITE

This website’s goal is to show Aki’s professional work and her services for potential clients. My work is research, design, and development. In terms of the website design, I wanted to express a warm look and feel like Aki’s latte artwork. To express that I decided to use the handcrafted elements for the design. One is a calligraphy font on titles, and another is Aki’s latte artwork image on the landing page. In addition, we used a pencil texture Illustration that Aki created.
