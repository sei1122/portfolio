FASHION RUNWAY POSTER

This project was required to use perspective grids, patterns, gradient meshes and blended shapes. The theme was a fashion runway. Perspective is used for the runway, wall, and patterned floor. Gradient meshes were used to make cloth and body textures. Blended shape was used for the star to circle design on the back wall.

YEAR: 2016

BY: Illustrator

PROJECT: Personal
