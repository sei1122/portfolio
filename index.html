About

I have been working as a product designer since 2016, and have worked across diverse platforms including consumer websites, enterprise tools, e-commerce sites, and mobile/tablet applications. My passion is to create designs that convey ideas and information that positively impact people, businesses, and organizations.

My belief in community led me to previously take an active role with UX Wizards, a large Bay Area UX group, where I led the design team responsible for developing the brand guide and website.

When I step away from designing, I love finding great coffee shops, exploring museums, hiking beautiful trails, and playing and chatting with my little son.
