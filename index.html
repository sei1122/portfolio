ABOUT

I have been working as a product designer since 2016, and worked on web based products and visual design. My passion is to create designs that convey ideas and information which positively impact people and business.

I’m on the executive board team for UX Wizards. This is a community of over 2800 designers. In this group, I lead the design and web team and help organize event 10s to 100s of people.

When I have time, I go to museums to see amazing artist’s works, casually draw illustrations with pen and paper, go hiking to enjoy scenery and find coffee shops :)
