ABOUT

I have been working as a product designer since 2016, and have worked on web based products and visual designs. My passion is to create designs that convey ideas and information that positively impact people, businesses, and organizations.

I believe in community, and am part of the executive board for a Bay Area UX Community, UX Wizards. There, I help manage 30 volunteers who host events for 3,200+ members. I’m also the Design team lead and led the creation of Brand Guide and website.

When I have time, I enjoy going to museums to see amazing artists’ works, casually drawing illustrations with pen and paper, going on hikes to enjoy the scenery and finding coffee shops. :)
