Hello, my name is Aki Tokuhara, welcome to Blue Palette. Started in 2016 with help from a good friend, this website is an introduction to some of my work with Latte Art, in particular the kind where you “etch”. Etching is a method where you use a pencil like utensil to literally “draw” directly on the foam layer of a Latte drink, creating intricate art that not only is fun to look, but tasty at the same time!

My hope is to use this website to connect with others who are interested in knowing more about this style of serving coffee, or are potentially looking for actual catering at events such as corporate seminars, meetings, or even home parties. If you are interested in reaching out, please feel free to use the email below to get in contact.

Thanks!
