Skincare Package Design

This is a 3 skincare product design project. The client’s idea was each product has stand-alone design but somehow unified. To obtain this, I decided to use the same color scheme and font so when those products are on a shelf, it looks still the same product line even designs are different.

Luna Charclay Mask

The design is started from Luna Charclay Mask. This design idea came from the name of Luna. I started brainstorming the different types of moon objects and came up with this Arabic style of the moon. Main moon design and the text is set to black to match the black tube cap.

Primrose

Primrose flower represents youth, new beginnings, and their beauty. My idea is to use the flower-like primrose element. For the front of the box package, I used off white flower elements because I set the focal point to the product name and the purpose of the skincare and try not to distract the focal point. The Side of the package design is more playful. Use the different sizes of flowers and added the dot looking of the floating flower in the between.

Eminence

I tried to express the superiority image for this product which is the meaning of “Eminence”. I also used the Arabic style design element for the label and box to unify with Luna Charclay and Primrose. The flower design on the label is made for a little surprise ;) When a customer opens the box, a flower is sitting in a box like a little bouquet.

PROJECT: Luna, Primrose, Eminence

YEAR: 2019

ROLE: Design

BY: Illustrator, Photoshop
